Results & Observations

Key insights and limitations observed during model development.

Model Performance

The model demonstrated stronger learning behavior for Expansion (EXP) grading compared to ICM and TE, which present more subtle visual features.

This observation aligns with clinical understanding, where expansion stage shows clearer morphological differences.

Prediction Consistency

Predictions were found to be consistent across similar embryo images, indicating that the model learned stable visual patterns rather than random noise.

Limitations

Performance is constrained by dataset size, annotation quality, and inherent subjectivity in grading labels. The model should be interpreted as a decision-support tool rather than a diagnostic system.

Future Improvements

Potential improvements include larger datasets, time-lapse embryo imaging, and explainability techniques to visualize which regions influence predictions.
