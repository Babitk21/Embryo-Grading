Business Goal

Reducing subjectivity and improving consistency in embryo grading.

Clinical Motivation

IVF success depends heavily on selecting the most viable embryo. Even experienced embryologists may disagree in grading outcomes, leading to variability in embryo selection decisions.

AI as Decision Support

This system is designed to assist clinicians rather than replace them. It provides a reproducible, consistent second opinion that supports training, research, and standardization in embryo grading workflows.
